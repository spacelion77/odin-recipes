Lasagna

This simple brick-oven pizza recipe has been made famous by several well-known, Brooklyn wood-fire pizzerias. Best by ice-cold, pilsner-style lager beer.

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1/4 cup warm water>
  • 1 teaspoon active dry yeast
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 tablespoon extra-virgin olive oil

Toppings:

  • 6 oz mozzarella cheese
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 cup canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 leaves fresh basil

Directions

  1. Step 1Make the dough
  2. Step 2Knead the dough
  3. Step 3Divide dough in half
  4. Step 4Make the pizzas
  5. Step 5Preheat the oven
  6. Step 6Prepare the pizza
