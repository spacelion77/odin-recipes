Lasagna

This chocolate Swiss roll is a simpler and smaller version of Beth Le Manach's bûche de Noël. It takes time but is worth the effort.

Ingredients

Cake:

  • 1/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/4 cup cocoa powder
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1/8 tsp baking soda
  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup white sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 2 1/2 Tbsp melted butter

Filling:

  • 3/4 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tsp powdered sugar
  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1/8 cup nutella

Directions

  1. Step 1Preheat oven to 325 degrees F
  2. Step 2Make cake
  3. Step 3Combine eggs and vanilla using mixer
  4. Step 4Bake in preheated oven for 8-9 min
  5. Step 5Roll cake into tube shape
  6. Step 6Make filling
  7. Step 7Unroll cake and spread filling, then roll cake
